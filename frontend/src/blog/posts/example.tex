# Getting Started with Moonstack

Welcome to Moonstack — where attention becomes revenue.

In today’s creator economy, attention is the most valuable asset. Moonstack helps you capture it, scale it, and monetize it automatically using AI-powered video automation.

## What is Moonstack?

Moonstack is an AI-driven media automation platform designed to transform information into high-performing video content — optimized for reach, engagement, and revenue.

Instead of manually scripting, editing, captioning, and publishing content, Moonstack automates the entire workflow — so you can focus on growth while the system works for you.

## Why Moonstack?

Moonstack is built for creators, brands, and media operators who want to:

- Scale content production without scaling costs  
- Turn long-form content into monetizable short-form videos  
- Automate captions, voiceovers, and formatting  
- Optimize content for engagement and platform performance  
- Convert audience attention into measurable revenue  

## Core Features

1. **AI Video Automation**  
   Automatically transform ideas, prompts, or source material into ready-to-publish videos.

2. **Smart Content Repurposing**

   Generate natural voiceovers and dynamic captions optimized for engagement.

4. **Revenue-Focused Optimization**

   Replace manual editing pipelines with intelligent automation.
